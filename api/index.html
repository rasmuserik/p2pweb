▸ dist(addr)

xor-distance between two addresses, - with 24 significant bits, and with an offset such that the distance between 0x000.. and 0x800... is 2 ** 123, and distance 0b1111.. and 0b1010111.. is 2**122 + 2**120. This also means that the distance can be represented within a single precision float. (with some loss on least significant bits)
